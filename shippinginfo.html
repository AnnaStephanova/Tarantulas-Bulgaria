Shipping, Returns & Replacements

where we ship?

We ship all around Europe and America.

We don't ship in Africa and Asia.

Notice!

Check if your local laws allow keeping exotic pets. If they don't we will cancel your order.

packing

We try our best to always package orders so that any tarantulas are as safe as possible from heat, cold, dehydration or from being crushed too easily. We use various boxes, wraps, insulating and stuffing materials to provide shock absorption and insulation that will ensure your parcel arrives in good condition.

shipping options

Shipping in Bulgaria

Courier- We ship using Speedy or Econt. The shipping costs are included in the final price.

Shipping Internationally

DHL- The shipping costs are included in the final price.

shipping

Orders placed during weekends or official public holidays will be shipped the first following business day or a day where no further delays will impede quick and safe delivery.

Please note that shipments cannot be delivered to post box addresses. We can only ship to a proper physical address like a physical company or home address.

Notice!

During excessive hot or colds spells by the weather, if Tarantulas-Bulgaria.com considers the weather to be a danger to any shipped creatures, Tarantulas-Bulgaria.com may withhold an order at it’s own discretion until we consider it safe for the order to be shipped. This will be communicated at our earliest convenience to the client via the online ordering system, email or sms at our discretion.

Shipping Risks

Shipping creatures and products will always carry risk on the side of the sender and the receiver. Tarantulas-Bulgaria.com nor any of it’s service providers can 100% guarantee that creatures will arrive at their destination safely, unharmed and in good health. In order to get parcels around the country as fast as possible, couriers use various methods of transport like cars, trucks, trains and aeroplanes via hundreds of pairs of hands in order to get the parcels to their destinations on time. Covering these long distances brings the risk of creatures suffering at the hands of these main threats; cold, heat, dehydration and or a destroyed parcel.

D.O.A (Dead on Arrival)

In a D.O.A. case where a creature has died during transit, Tarantulas-Bulgaria.com will once per shipped order, consider crediting the customer 50% of the price of the creature/s in question.

Exclusion

We are prepared to replace a creature that has died in transit as long as the cause of the of the death is due to poor packaging or handling by us or a courier service we have used. But in a case where deliveries do not go as planned due to matters out of our control, i.e., national strikes, political / public unrest, acts of God, severe weather and other causes that are totally out of our control, we can not be held liable for any loss whatsoever.
