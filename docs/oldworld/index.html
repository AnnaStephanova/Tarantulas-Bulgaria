Characteristics

Old World species of tarantulas lack urticating hairs of their New World counterparts and will therefore use their fangs and often “medically significant” venom as defense. Although a bite from an Old World species won’t kill you, it can cause excruciating pain, dizziness, full body crampings, and nausea. These fast ‘n feisty spiders demand a bit more cauting and experience to care for, and many have more complicated husbandry needs. They should never be handled
