Pelinobus muticus

King Baboon

Common names:King Baboon Tarantula

Adult Size:BIG 18cm to 20cm

Type: Old World, Fossorial.

Growth RateSlow

Temperament: Very defensive and bold. Can be a pet hole.

Lifespan: Females (20 to 25 years), Males (10 to 15 years)

Recommended Climate:Kenya. 26-30°C (day), 22-24°C (night).

Basic Enclosure Requirements:Fossorial setup with at least 4 x the size of the spider in horizontal space, 15cm to 20cm (or more) of substrate to allow for burrowing with the customary shelter and a water dish.
