Cyriopagopus sp. Hati Hati

Purple Earth Tiger

Common names:Purple Earth Tiger. Hati Hati Earth Tiger

Adult Size:18 to 20cm

Type: Old World, Arboreal

Growth RateFast

Temperament: Defensive and aggressive

Lifespan: Females (14 to 15yrs), Males (3 to 4 years)

Recommended Climate: Indonesia. 20-22°C (Night) 24-28°C (Day)

Basic Enclosure Requirements:Arboreal setup with at least 4 x the size of the spider in vertical space, 5cm to 10cm of substrate, a vertical shelter like hollow cork bark or bamboo and a customary water dish.
